Common Questions

How is my instant quote calculated?

Pricing is based on bedrooms, bathrooms, service type, and frequency. You'll see your price before you submit anything.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not at all. Many customers provide entry instructions and go about their day. All cleaners are background-checked.

What's included in a make-ready / turnover clean?

Full interior clean, inside appliances and cabinets, baseboards, and a final walk-through checklist so the space is ready to show or move into.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes, just give us 24 hours notice and we'll move things around at no charge.
